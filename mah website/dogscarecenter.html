A Word of Caution:

Resellers of Dogs & Puppies are sometimes requesting for Puppies for Free Adoption, through the Classifieds. DogsIndia.com cannot verify all the requests. Puppy/Dog Owners are kindly advised to check for themselves whether the requests are genuine. Please Adopt Indian Street Puppies. Take care of them, right from their childhood when they are Puppies, Vaccinate them regularly & completely. You will start Loving them, if you adopt them as Puppies. We have numerous Indian street Dogs with No security from Heat, Cold, Rain.

Adopt them with an open heart.
